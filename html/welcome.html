DISCOGS ENHANCER

QUICK START GUIDE

Thanks for installing Discogs Enhancer! I've been building and maintaining this open source project since 2016. If you need help with it please email me at discogs.enhancer@gmail.com. I'll usually respond within a day or two.

If you don't see the record icon in the Chrome toolbar follow these 3 steps to add it:

Step One:

Click the puzzle piece icon. This will open a list of all the extensions currently installed in your browser. Look through it to find Discogs Enhancer.

Step Two:

Once you've found Discogs Enhancer on the list - click the pin icon to keep Discogs Enhancer in Chrome's toolbar.

Step Three:

Click the record icon to show the Discogs Enhancer menu. From here you can enable and disable features. Hover over the question mark icons (?) to see a brief explanation of each feature. Click LEARN for in-depth instructions.

Questions? Comments? Have a suggestion? Email me at discogs.enhancer@gmail.com!
